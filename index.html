For detailed info and roadmap checkout: The Eclectic Meme Conspiracy Design Document

The Eclectime Conspiracy is the first of many different games & virtual worlds to be built using this engine.
What is JiGS? A php/mysql/html5 engine for building online RPGS/Trading Games and multiplayer turn based games. Usages vary from MMORPGs to Business war gaming and decision based training, Game based learning and gamification of content (Joomla, Drupal and other php based CMSs).
